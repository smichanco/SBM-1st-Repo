Recipe for Good Popcorn

Ingredients:
Organic Kernals, butter-flavored coconut oil, and salt. Optional: Clarified butter

Place oil in pot, enough to cover the bottom and put stove on medium
Place a single kernal in the pot, once kernal pops, pour the rest of the kernals in
Cover pot with paper towel and then the lid, leaving room for moisture to escape
Once popping stops, remove pot from stove, and add salt/butter to taste
